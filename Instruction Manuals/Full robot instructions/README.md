# Bellow you will find the instructions to re construct our robot from scratch.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> ## Before opening the manual, ensure you have all the necessary [equiptment and materials](https://github.com/VIVA-LA-VIDA-Hellas/VIVA-LA-VIDA/blob/main/Instruction%20Manuals/Materials%20needed%20and%20BOM.md), and have prepared the [Lego ackerman](https://github.com/VIVA-LA-VIDA-Hellas/VIVA-LA-VIDA/tree/main/Instruction%20Manuals/Lego%20ackerman%20instructions) and [Lego differential](https://github.com/VIVA-LA-VIDA-Hellas/VIVA-LA-VIDA/tree/main/Instruction%20Manuals/Lego%20differential%20instructions). If you do not have all materials and/or the lego parts have not yet been built, you will be unable to finish following these instructions.
